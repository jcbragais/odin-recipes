Pizza

Description

Make a classic, crispy-chewy homemade pizza in under 2 hours. This recipe yields two 12-inch pizzas. For the perfect crust, you will need active dry yeast, bread flour, extra-virgin olive oil, a quality tomato sauce, and whole-milk mozzarella.

Ingredients:

    For the Dough:

  • 1 1/3 cups warm water (105° F - 110° F)
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4) active dry or instant yeast
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups bread flour (or unbleached all-purpose flour)
  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

    • For the Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Cornmeal (for dusting)
  • Your choice of meats or vegetables (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Make the dough: Mix the dough ingredients together by hand or use a hand-held or stand mixer. Do this in steps as described in the written recipe below.
  2. KneadKnead by hand or with your mixer. I like doing this by hand. If you’re new to yeasted doughs, my How to Knead Dough post and video can help with this step.
  3. RisePlace dough into a greased mixing bowl, cover tightly, and set aside to rise for about 90 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Punch & ShapePunch & shape: Punch down risen dough to release air bubbles. Divide in 2. Roll dough out into a 12-inch circle. Cover and rest as you prep the pizza toppings.
  5. Top ItTop with favorite pizza toppings.
  6. BakeBake pizza at a very high temperature for only about 15 minutes.
